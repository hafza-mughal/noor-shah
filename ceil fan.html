ceiling fans

A ceiling fan, with its direction of rotation
set so that the warmer air on the ceiling is pushed down along the walls and into the room, heating the cooler air.
This avoids blowing a stream of air directly at the occupants of the room
which would tend to cool them..

  • The size of the table fan depends on the area you want to cool. For smaller rooms or personal use, a compact table fan with a blade size of 12 to 16 inches may suffice. For larger rooms, consider a table fan with a blade size of 18 inches or more for better air circulation.

  • To clean your table fan, first, make sure it is unplugged. Use a soft brush or vacuum cleaner to remove dust from the grill and blades. You can also use a damp cloth to wipe down the surface. Avoid using harsh chemicals or water directly on the motor.

  • Our table fans are designed to operate quietly, providing you with a comfortable environment without unnecessary noise disturbances. However, the noise level may vary depending on the speed setting and model of the fan.

  • Yes, most of our table fans come with multiple speed settings, allowing you to adjust the airflow according to your preference and comfort level.

  • While table fans are generally safe to use unattended, it's advisable to turn them off when leaving the room for an extended period to prevent any potential hazards.
